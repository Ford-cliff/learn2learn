Course Catalog

Browse all subjects available on the platform, organized by college, program, year level, and semester. Use this to see what subjects you can be matched on before you sign in.

Step 01

Browse the Catalog

Find your college, program, and year level here. Each subject listed is one you can declare as a strength or a subject you need help with.

Step 02

Declare Your Subjects

After logging in, select which subject you excel at and which one you are struggling with. The system uses these to find your ideal peer match.

Step 03

Get Matched and Learn

The platform connects you with a compatible peer — someone strong where you need help, and who needs help where you are strong. Then you learn together.
