OUR MISSION

About Learn2Learn

At Palawan State University, students often face a common challenge: some struggle with subjects because they learn at a different pace, have limited time with instructors, or simply lack a peer group to support them. Meanwhile, others excel in those very same subjects — yet there's no central place for these students to find each other.

Learn2Learn was created to bridge that gap. It is a PSU-exclusive peer-assisted learning platform where students can identify subjects they are strong and weak at, connect with compatible learning partners, and collaborate through a shared communication system.

Project Objectives

  • ✅ Build a login system exclusive to PSU email accounts
  • ✅ Let students from all programs declare their academic strengths and weaknesses
  • ✅ Simulate subject-based student matching between compatible peers
  • ✅ Provide a prototype peer-to-peer communication platform
  • ✅ Design an accessible, user-friendly web environment for PSU students
